No Inherited Musical Talent Desire & Hard Work

There is a phrase that says hard works outshines natural talent any day. This is not true. What is true however, is that if you put in the work to learn a new hobby or find ways to improve an old skills, you will see an improvement. No one in my family is muscially talented. Not one.


None of us are not creative or artsy, and even though we all wished we could sing better or play an instrument, we can't. Yet Faith and not giving up has allowed me to say that even the unmusically talented can achieve thier dreams. I have been blessed to learn how to play, even to the point where others can enjoy the music I play.

A fallen passion 60-0 MPH In just 1 year

One of my biggest hobbies after my mission was one thing and one thing only-- race cars. All I thought about was driving everywhere, traveling, and having a good time with cars. It was the feel, the adrenaline, and the social status that got me hooked onto the car scene. That all came to an end in 2019. I was in a car crash that totalled the car and my dreams for bieng a rally race car driver. Tragic as it was and shortlived as my love for cars was, I am glad I lived it up. I am glad I got to love.
